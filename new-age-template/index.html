Leonardo DiCaprio

Leonardo DiCaprio is one of my all-time favorite actors. His extensive range of characters have been memorable across his entire career. Below are my top 3 favorite roles of his.

Synopsis

The Wolf of Wall Street (2013)

Jordan Belfort (Leonardo DiCaprio) is a young, full of hopes, ambitious man who is looking forward to starting his first job on Wall Street.

He immediately gets impressed by the world of investment and by his new boss, Mark Hanna (Matthew McConaughey) who takes him out for a lunch and gives him tips on how to be a better, more efficient seller and earn more money. Unfortunately, Jordan loses his job after the stock markets crashes on infamous Black Monday and therefore, he becomes unemployed again. He decides to take a job as a stockbroker at a boiler room and after being the most successful employee, together with his colleague, Donnie Azoff (Jonah Hill), he sets up a company that has its income from defrauding shareholders. Finally, Jordan has a life that he has always dreamt of: he is one of the richest people in the world and he can spend his money on anything he wants, including drugs, jets, cars and prostitutes. As it was to be expected, FBI quickly finds out about his scams, Jordan, however, has a few ideas how to deceive them.

The Aviator (2004)

The story of aviation pioneer Howard Hughes (Leonardo DiCaprio), the eccentric billionaire industrialist and Hollywood film mogul, famous for romancing some of the world's most beautiful women.

The drama recounts the years of his life from the late 1920s through the 1940s, an epoch when Hughes was directing and producing Hollywood movies and test flying innovative aircrafts he designed and created. It also follows his descent into madness as his compulsiveness for cleanliness gives way to frequent outbursts and ticks. He turns away those closest to him, confining himself and emerging only when absolutely necessary.

Catch Me If You Can (2002)

A true story about Frank Abagnale Jr. who, before his 19th birthday, successfully conned millions of dollars worth of checks as a Pan Am pilot, doctor, and legal prosecutor.

An FBI agent makes it his mission to put him behind bars. But Frank not only eludes capture, he revels in the pursuit.

Reviews

The story of Jordan Belfort and his get rich quick scheme was brilliantly told, filmed, and acted. Greed and Excessivness was the overarching theme. Leonardo DiCaprio was at the top of his game in this film.

The Aviator is a masterpiece in my humble opinion. DiCaprio shines in this film and is very convivncing in his portrayal of Howard Hughes.

The story of Frank Abagnail was very interesting, captivating and perfectly acted by Mr. DiCaprio.
